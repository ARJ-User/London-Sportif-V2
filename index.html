About London Sportif

More Than a Club

London Sportif was established in 2017 in the East End. Our organisation was created with the intention of targeting both the youth and adults through diverse sports activities that build character, community and confidence.

We run three thriving sports clubs — Badminton, Cricket and Football — all of which take place regularly on both weekdays and weekends. Alongside adult badminton, we hold dedicated junior sessions to nurture the next generation of athletes.

Our vision extends further: to widen our sports community and launch women's sports sessions, ensuring every member of our community has a place in our family.

Our Story

About London Sportif

London Sportif was established in 2017 in the East End of London. We were created with a clear mission: to use sport as a vehicle for community cohesion, youth development and social integration.

Our community reaches across backgrounds, ages and abilities. We have grown from a small local initiative into a multi-sport registered charity — and we are just getting started.

We operate three sports clubs (Badminton, Cricket and Football), hold junior sessions, and actively participate in local tournaments and leagues.

Our future plans include launching dedicated women's sports sessions and continuing to expand our reach across the city.

About the Club

The Beautiful Game

Our football club competes in local East London leagues and participates in community tournaments throughout the season. We train regularly on both weekdays and weekends, welcoming players from all backgrounds and abilities.

We are affiliated with The Football Association (FA) and follow all FA guidelines for safe and inclusive football. Our coaching staff are DBS-checked and hold relevant FA coaching badges.

Whether you're a seasoned player or just getting into football, come join us. Training sessions are open — just bring your boots.

About the Club

Bat, Bowl, Belong

Smash, Rally, Win

Junior Programme

Young Champions

Our Junior Badminton Team is dedicated to developing young talent in East London. Our structured sessions combine fun with skill development, helping juniors build confidence on and off the court.

All coaches are DBS-checked, trained in safeguarding, and hold relevant sports coaching qualifications.

Parents are welcome to watch sessions and get involved in the club community.

🛡️ Safeguarding Commitment

London Sportif is fully committed to safeguarding the welfare of all young people. All sessions comply with Badminton England and Sport England safeguarding policies.
